Neptune

God of the Sea

Distance from Sun: 30


Moons: 16 moons, largest being Triton,
Avg. Surface Temp: -330°F
Composition: 80% or more is made up of dense, fluid "icy" materials, such as methane, water, and ammonia. Scientists think there may be a hot ocean under Neptune's cloud cover
Diameter of Planet: 30,775 miles
