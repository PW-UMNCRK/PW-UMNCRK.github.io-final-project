Ursa Minor

The Little Bear

Number of Stars: 7
Brightest Star: Polaris
Also known as: The little dipper

Arcas was the son of Zeus and Callisto. When he grew up, he became a fearsome hunter. However, he nearly shot his Mom, not recognizing her as a bear. So, Zeus turned him into a bear as well and placed both Arcas and Callisto in the sky as constellations.
