Orion

The Hunter

Number of Stars: 7
Brightest Star: Rigel
Also known as: Yes! Orion's Belt

Orion was a great hunter. He was good friends with Artemis, Goddess of the Hunt and Wilderness. He is most famous for his death, of which there is multiple versions. The most common is that he boasted to Artemis that he could hunt and kill every animal in the world. Overhearing this, Mother Earth got upset and sent a scorpion to kill him before he could. The scorpion suceeded and Artemis, mourning her friend, placed him in the stars.
