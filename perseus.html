Perseus

Hero of Seriphos

Number of Stars: 19
Brightest Star: Mirfak

Perseus was the son of King Zeus, and a mortal princess of Argos named Danae. Danae's father, King Acrisius, had recieved a prophecy saying that he would be killed by his grandson one day. So, he puts Danae and the infant Perseus into a wooden chest, and the two would eventully end up on an island called Seriphos. Years later, the king of this island lusts after Danae, but all his attempts are blocked by Perseus, now of age, as he believes him to be a dishonorable man. So, to get Perseus off his back, the king sends him to fight the Gorgons, monsters with snakes for hair, and bring back one of their heads. Specifically Medusa's head, as she is the only mortal one. Perseus manages it with the help of a couple assorted gods. On his way back, he does a sidequest or two and does actually end up fulfilling the prophecy of him killing his grandfather.
