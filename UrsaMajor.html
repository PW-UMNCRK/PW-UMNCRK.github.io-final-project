Ursa Major

The Big Bear

Number of Stars: 7
Brightest Star: Alioth
Also known as: The big dipper.

The King of Gods, Zeus, fell in love with the nymph Callisto, who was a follower of Artemis. Zeus seduced Callisto and made her pregnant. She was then turned into a bear. In some stories, Zeus turned her into a bear to protect her from his jealous wife. In others, Artemis turns Callisto into a bear as punishment for breaking her vow of chastity. She was later placed as a constellation to protect her from hunters.
