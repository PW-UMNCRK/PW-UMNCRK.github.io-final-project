Pegasus

The Winged Horse

Number of Stars: 9
Brightest Star: Enif
Also known as: The great square of pegasus.

When Perseus beheaded Medusa, Pegasus sprung from her blood. Pegasus was a winged, wild horse. He got tamed by a man named Bellerophron. Bellerophrons success in this, and other less important success, made the hero decide to fly up to Mount Olympus, home of the gods, on Pegasus's back. The gods, not liking this display of hubris, sent a gadfly to sting Pegasus. After getting stung, the startled horse bucked Bellerophron off, and he fell back to the Earth and got severely injured.
