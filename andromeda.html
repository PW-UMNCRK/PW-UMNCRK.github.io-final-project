Andromeda

The Chained Woman

Number of Stars: 16
Brightest Star: Alpheratz

Andromeda was the Aethiopia (Ethiopia), daughter of King Cepheus and Queen Cassiopeia. Cassiopeia bragged that her daughter was more beautiful than the Nereids, sea nymphs blessed with incredible beauty. The nereids then demanded that Cassiopeia be punished for this, so the God of the Sea sent a sea monster to attack the kingdom. Cepheus was told by an oracle that the only way to save his kingdom was to chain his daughter, Andromeda, to a rock as a sacrifice. So he did. Andromeda was then saved by the hero Perseus, who slayed the sea monster. They later married.
