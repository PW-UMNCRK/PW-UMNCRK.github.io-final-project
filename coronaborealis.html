Corona Borealis

The Northern Crown

Number of Stars: 7
Brightest Star: Alpha Coronae Borealis (sometimes called Alphekka)

In Greek mythology, Ariadne was the princess of Crete. Upon being abandoned on an island, she meets Dionysus, God of Wine and Revelry. She wore the crown at their marriage, and Dionysus put the crown's jewels in the sky as start to commemorate their love.
